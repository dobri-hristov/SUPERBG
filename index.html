Year: 2014

Power: 510 hp

City: Plovdiv

A model unique to Mercedes-AMG, the GT S is a pure two-seat sportscar that makes few if any compromises in terms of performance. Powered by a new twin-turbo V8, it’s supercar fast and racetrack ready, yet it spoils its occupants with a luxurious cabin, decent space, excellent levels of refinement and the sort of safety and amenity systems you’d expect from a top-line Mercedes. AMG insists it's not targeting 911, but we think it’s got very close to the bullseye. Bellissimo! Magnifico! Stupendo! All of these exclamatory Italian adjectives apply to the positively beautiful Ferrari Roma.
